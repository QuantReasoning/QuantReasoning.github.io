About Me

Data Analytics Projects

Penguin E-Book Pricing Analysis and Prediction
EDA by filtering Penguin books from the dataset, cleaning and preprocessing key features: price, ratings, and reviews; visualising the distributions of price and ratings, and extracting important keywords from book titles using TF-IDF to see patterns influencing pricing. Built a Tableau Dashboard and an ML model: a Random Forest Regressorto predict e-book prices based on ratings, reviews, and title keywords.
QSAR SARS-CoV
QSAR model using Random Forest regression to predict molecular bioactivity (pIC₅₀) from ECFP4 fingerprints. EDA to check relationships between data: bioactivity, distribution IC60/pIC50, molecular descriptors. The workflow includes data preprocessing, stratified splitting, and feature importance analysis to highlight key substructures influencing activity.
SQL projects
A set of SQL projects designed to sharpen my data querying and management skills.
Patient Risk Stratification (in R)
EDA for visualising key variables: age distribution, diagnosis counts, daily admissions over time, relationship between treatment cost and length of stay. Created a synthetic clinical dataset and implemented a multinomial logistic regression model in R to stratify patients into Low, Medium, and High risk groups based on age, diagnosis, and length of stay; The model achieved an overall accuracy of 88.3%.
